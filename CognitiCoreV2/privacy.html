Privacy Policy

1. PURPOSE OF THIS PRIVACY POLICY

This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) outlines how Cogniti Core LLC (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, processes, and protects your personal data when you interact with our website (cogniticore.com) (“Site”) and use our services (“Services”). Our business primarily serves clients in the United States, Canada, and India, and we ensure compliance with data privacy regulations applicable to these regions.

This Policy is designed to help you make informed decisions when using our Site and Services. Please read it carefully. It is intended to complement our Cookie Policy.

  • United States: California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and other state privacy laws.
  • Canada: Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA).
  • India: Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP Act).

For other privacy regulations, please refer to our General Privacy Policy.

2. SCOPE OF THIS PRIVACY POLICY

This Policy applies to the personal data we collect directly through your interactions with us, our Site, or our Services. It does not apply to third-party websites or services that may be linked to or referenced on our Site. For employees, contractors, and other personnel of Cogniti Core LLC, a separate Employee Privacy Notice governs the handling of your personal data.

3. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, applicable laws, or regulations. You will be notified of any significant changes through our Site or by email, and we will update the ‘Last Updated’ date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Policy regularly.

4. WHO WE ARE

Cogniti Core LLC specializes in providing AI-driven IT solutions and services. We serve clients primarily in the United States, Canada, and India, and act as the data controller when processing your personal data as described in this Policy. In certain cases, when processing data on behalf of our clients, we may act as a data processor. This Policy will clarify when this distinction applies.
